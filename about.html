About Us

Magnolia Insurance Group is an independent insurance agency, free to help you choose the best carrier for your insurance needs. We do not work for an insurance company; we work for you. When you have a claim, we stand with you and follow through to see that you receive fair, prompt payment and service. Magnolia represents a carefully selected group of financially strong, reputable insurance companies, allowing us to offer the best coverage at competitive prices.
